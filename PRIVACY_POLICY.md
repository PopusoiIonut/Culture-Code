# Privacy Policy for CultureCode

**Last Updated: June 29, 2026**

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This privacy policy explains how **CultureCode** (referred to as "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## 1. No Personal Data Collection
We believe in absolute privacy. 
- **No Personal Data Collected**: The App does not collect, record, transmit, or monitor any of your personal data, accounts, search history, or device identifiers.
- **No User Profiles**: There are no account creations, user profiles, or logins required to use the App.
- **No Third-Party Advertising**: We do not sell, trade, or share any information with third parties or data brokers.

---

## 2. Data Storage & Local Cache
- **100% Offline Database**: The pre-loaded countries database is completely offline and bundled within the App.
- **Local Document Storage**: Any country rules you search and download online are stored securely and exclusively on your physical device (`SavedCountries.json`). No travel details or search history are sent to our servers.

---

## 3. Network Queries
To download information for new destinations, the App makes direct network requests:
- **Wikipedia API**: Queries Wikipedia to fetch public, open-source articles regarding local culture and etiquette for grounding.
- **Google Gemini API**: Uses Google's generative AI models to synthesize and structure rules. This query is sent using the developer API key configured in the App.
- **Privacy of Searches**: These queries contain only the requested country name and Wikipedia text. No personal identifiers, location data, or user-specific metadata are attached to these network calls.

---

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
